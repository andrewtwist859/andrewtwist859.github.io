Advice to humans

from The Humans by Matt Haig

1. Shame is a shackle. Free yourself.

2. Don’t worry about your abilities. You have the ability to love. That is enough.

3. Be nice to other people. At a universal level, they are you.

4. Technology won’t save mankind. Humans will.

5. Laugh. It suits you.

6. Be curious. Question everything. A present fact is just a future fiction.

7. Irony is fine, but not as fine as feeling.

8. Sometimes to be yourself you will have to forget yourself and become something else. Your character is not a fixed thing. You will sometimes have to move to keep up with it.

9. You shouldn’t have been born. Your existence is as close to impossible as can be. To dismiss the impossible is to dismiss yourself.

10. The road to snobbery is the road to misery. And vice versa.

11. Tragedy is just comedy that hasn’t come to fruition. One day we will laugh at this. We will laugh at everything.

12. Happiness is not out here, it is in there.

13. New technology on Earth, just means something that we will laugh at in five years. Value the stuff you won’t laugh at in five years. Like love. Or a good poem. Or a song. Or the sky.

14. If there is a sunset, stop and look at it. Knowledge is finite. Wonder is infinite.

15. Don’t aim for perfection. Evolution, and life, only happen through mistakes.

16. Don’t always try to be cool. The whole universe is cool. It’s the warm bits that matter.

17. Walt Whitman was right about at least one thing. You will contradict yourself. You are large. You contain multitudes.

18. No one is ever completely right about anything. Anywhere.

19. Everyone is a comedy. If people are laughing at you they just don’t quite understand the joke that is themselves.

20. Your brain is open. Never let it be closed.

21. In a thousand years, if humans survive that long, everything you know will have been disproved. And replaced by even bigger myths.

22. You have the power to stop time. You do it by kissing. Or listening to music. Music, by the way, is how you see things you can’t otherwise see. It is the most advanced thing you have. It is a superpower.

23. The things you don’t need to live (books, art, cinema, wine and so on) are the things you need to live.

24. No two moralities match. Accept different shapes, so long as they aren’t sharp enough to hurt.

25. At some point, bad things are going to happen. Have someone to hold on to.

26. If you are laughing, check that you don’t really want to cry. And vice versa.

27. There are a lot of idiots in your species. Lots and lots. You are not one of them. Hold your ground.

28. It is not the length of life that matters. It’s the depth. But while burrowing, keep the sun above you.

29. Obey your head. Obey your heart. Obey your gut. In fact, obey everything except commands.

30. One day, if you get into a position of power, tell people this: just because you can, it doesn’t mean you should. There is a power and a beauty in unproved conjectures, unkissed lips and unpicked flowers.

31. Start fires. But only metaphorically. Unless you are cold and it’s a safe setting. In which case: start fires.

32. Most humans don’t think about things very much. They survive by thinking about needs and wants alone. But you are not one of them. Be careful.

33. No one will understand you. It is not, ultimately, that important. What is important is that you understand you.

34. Politeness is often fear. Kindness is always courage. But caring is what makes you human. Care more, become more human.

35. You get up. You put on your clothes. And then you put on your personality. Choose wisely.

36. You are more than the sum of your particles. And that is quite a sum.

37. Dark matter is needed to hold galaxies together. Your mind is a galaxy. More dark than light. But the light makes it worthwhile.

38. Men are not from Mars. Women are not from Venus. Do not fall for categories. Everyone is everything. Every ingredient inside a star is inside you, and every personality that ever existed competes in the theatre of your mind for the main role.

39. As a black hole forms it creates an immense gamma-ray burst, blinding whole galaxies with light and destroying millions of worlds. You could disappear at any second. This one. Or this one. Or this one. Make sure, as often as possible, you are doing something you’d be happy to die doing.

40. Be alive. That is your supreme duty to the world.
