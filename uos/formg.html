Ethnic origin

Please select the term you feel describes your ethnic origin. If none of the terms seem appropriate, select 'Other ethnic background'. If you want to withhold this information, select 'Information withheld'.

This information is treated confidentially. It is not passed on to the academic staff considering your application. We use it to keep track of the numbers of students joining us from each ethnic group. This helps us promote equality and diversity. Details of our Equal Opportunities policy.
