If you have questions you need answering before booking, this might help …

How do I travel to Sheffield?

By car, train, bus or tram - we’re easy to get to. You can find travel information online.

And if you meet one of our Access+ criteria, your travel can be refunded.

What happens at an open day?

Course talks, tours of our campus and guaranteed accommodation, a chance to talk to current students and much more.

What if I can’t attend?

Join one of our weekly online Sheffield Live events, book a campus tour or take a self-guided tour. There are loads of ways to visit us, both in person and online.

You can also visit our dedicated undergraduate study page. You’ll find the right course for you and all of the relevant info in our course listings, as well as our students talking about how their Sheffield degrees helped their future careers.

How can I get in touch?

There are many ways to contact us. You can even chat with a current student of your choice. Our students have heaps of Sheffield wisdom - so don’t hesitate to ask the niche questions.
