Applying to Sheffield

Support at every step

Applications are pretty straightforward. In most cases, you can do it through our website. Check online for key dates and deadlines and start your application — you don’t need to have completed your undergraduate studies to apply.

Watch our applying for postgraduate study talk

Watch our 'Applying for postgraduate study' video to learn more about the application process at the University of Sheffield. This 11-minute talk covers what to do before you apply, the application process itself, and what happens after you've submitted your application.

You can track the progress of your application

Once submitted, you can log in to track progress at any time. We’ll also stay in touch with you all the way through — emailing about important dates and information.

If you need extra support, please tell us

We’re here to help, from application to graduation.

Contact the Disability and Dyslexia Support Service

If you’re coming to study from outside the UK

You will likely be required to apply for a Student visa to study in the UK. If your application is successful, we’ll provide you with a Confirmation of Acceptance for Studies (CAS) number to show you are sponsored by an approved education provider for your visa application.

English language skills are essential

You need to prove your English is good enough to successfully complete your course. We accept many qualifications as proof. And if you need help, the University’s English Language Teaching Centre (ELTC) can help you improve your English to meet our entry requirements.
