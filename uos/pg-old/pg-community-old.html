Keeping you safe

SafeZone is a free app for students which provides round-the-clock safety reassurance from your phone. Using the app, you can alert University Security Services if you ever need help while on campus.

Sport & Physical Activity at Sheffield

We have 60+ clubs, from American football and boxing to mountaineering. Complete beginner, playing for fun or elite athletes — University Sport & Physical Activity Sheffield has you covered.

Number one Students’ Union in the UK

Ethical, sustainable and representing you, Sheffield Students’ Union is powered by students. It gives you a say on everything from the disposable coffee cup levy to the bigger picture of University policy. It’s run by a team of student representatives, including a Postgraduate Committee and a dedicated Postgraduate Students’ Officer.

It’s also where you’ll find some of the best gigs, events and nights out in the city — and many of our support services. No wonder students voted it the best SU seven years running.

Your Students’ Union

During my time as Postgraduate Officer, I’ve established a Postgraduate Welcome Week, ran the first Postgraduate Ball and trialled the first postgraduate taught student forum.

Your Students’ Union has a dedicated Postgraduate Officer. They represent and promote the views of postgraduate students across both the Students’ Union and the University.

Postgraduate Welcome Week is the first week of October, with a range of activities run by the Students’ Union to enable postgraduates to meet one another. Give It A Go also runs activities especially for postgraduates to allow you to meet others.
