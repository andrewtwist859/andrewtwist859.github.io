Daily support

Your wellbeing matters

When you feel happy, healthy and secure, you can truly focus on what matters to you. Our dedicated support teams are here to give you free and confidential advice. We’re always ready to listen.

On campus

For practical help

We’re trained to help you handle everything from housing and money, to immigration and childcare.

For families

The Students’ Union runs an Ofsted-registered nursery. The fees are subsidised according to family income.

For students with disabilities or difficulties

If you are studying with a disability, or a learning difficulty like dyslexia, you will find us ready to help with practical and personal support.

For physical and mental health

Register for free healthcare, get mental health support, or speak to any of our team of specialists who are trained to help with a range of issues.

For spiritual health

When and if you need them, our chaplains and Religious Advisers offer care and support to people of all faiths — and none.

Dedicated support for international students

We’re here for you before you arrive in Sheffield and throughout your course. We even offer a meet-and-greet service at Manchester Airport.

For visas and immigration

You can speak to a dedicated team for visa information and advice. There is also helpful information including self-help tools and step-by-step guidance on the visa application process. Simply search ‘immigration’ on our website.

For improving your English skills

You can get help with your English before and during your studies. If your partner or spouse comes with you to Sheffield, they can take lessons at the English Language Teaching Centre, too.
