Section F: Relevant Employment

Please provide details of your employment within the last three years.

Adding details of more than one job Enter your first job details and then click on 'Save And Continue'. Then click on 'Add New Employment Details' and repeat the process.

No employment within the last three years? If you have not had any employment in the last three years leave this section blank and click on 'Continue Without Saving'.

Items marked with an asterisk * must be completed.
