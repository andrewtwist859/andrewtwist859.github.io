1. Monday 16 September 2024
    Applications open for courses starting in September 2025
  2. Monday 23 December 2024 – Wednesday 1 January 2025
    University closed for Christmas
  3. June 2025
    For applicants who need a visa to study in the UK, CAS application begins
  4. Monday 18 August 2025
    If your course requires ATAS clearance, we advise you to apply for ATAS clearance by this date
  5. Early September 2025
    Online Module Selection opens in the Pre-Registration system for those students who are required to choose approved modules.
  6. Monday 1 September 2025
    Last date to request a deferral to September 2026 entry. You can request a deferral via your online application.
  7. Monday 1 September 2025
    Last date to apply for a postgraduate course if you'll need a visa to study in the UK
  8. Friday 5 September 2025
    Applications close for September 2025 entry
  9. Monday 8 September 2025
    We advise you to apply for your CAS number by this date. If you apply for your CAS number after this date and will be applying for your visa from outside the UK, we would advise you to use a priority visa application service.

    We won't arrange a CAS number if you apply for your CAS number less than 3 weeks before the latest possible start date for your course, unless you tell us you'll be applying for your visa inside the UK, or using a priority visa application service.

  10. Monday 15–Friday 19 September 2025
    Orientation Week
  11. Monday 22 September 2025
    Last date to let us know that you've met any offer conditions. If you'll need a visa to study in the UK, please note that you need to meet any offer conditions before you apply for your CAS number, and we advise you to apply for your CAS number by Monday 8 September 2025.
  12. Monday 22 September 2025
    Welcome Week, including registration at the University
  13. Monday 29 September 2025
    Teaching begins
  14. Monday 20 October 2025
    Latest possible start date for most courses (exceptions are listed on our New Students webpages)
