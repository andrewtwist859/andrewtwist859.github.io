Fees and budget friendly living

Fees

The most up-to-date information on home and international undergraduate fees can be found online. Government loans are available for eligible home fee-paying students to cover tuition fees and contribute to living costs.

Undergraduate fees and funding

Scholarships and bursaries

Some home fee-paying students can get bursaries. This is money from the University in addition to government funding. You may also be eligible to apply for a scholarship. In 2023/24 we dedicated over £8.5m in bursaries and scholarships to help home students, and awarded over £7.6m in fee scholarships to international students.

Part-time jobs

It’s common to supplement your income as a student with a part-time job. Even better, our Careers and Employability Service can help you find work.

How do I live my best student life on a budget?

It’s easy to live on a student budget in Sheffield with:

  1. Affordable rent
  2. Paid work as an Ambassador for the University
  3. Student discounts on nights out, food and drink
  4. Budget-friendly supermarkets
  5. Part-time jobs in the city
