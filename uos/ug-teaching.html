Top 3 differences between school and uni
  1. Uni work is a lot more independent and you dive deeper into the subjects that you love. I can personalise my assignments and research to focus on my interests!
  2. Even though it’s more independent, you can easily access one-to-one support. I’ve built a network of support with academic staff who help with all aspects of my learning.
  3. Uni teaching can be more accessible for disabled and neurodiverse students. I can study in more diverse and unique ways.
