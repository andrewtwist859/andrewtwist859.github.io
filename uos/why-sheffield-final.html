Researching universities can be difficult …

Which is why we’re giving you three excellent reasons why you’ll love the University of Sheffield!

  1. You’ll gain a degree from the top university in the Russell Group – important for future undergrads like you.
  2. You’ll find your community in an affordable, welcoming, culturally diverse university city located inside a national park. Even better, your accommodation is guaranteed.
